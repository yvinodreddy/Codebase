## TAX OPTIMIZATION WITH ICHRA

### What is ICHRA?
Individual Coverage Health Reimbursement Arrangement - allows you to reimburse employee's individual insurance premiums tax-free.

### How It Works:
1. Rajesh buys individual insurance: $719.92/month (with subsidies)
2. Company reimburses via ICHRA: $72/month tax-free (10% of subsidized premium)
3. Rajesh receives reimbursement tax-free
4. Company gets business tax deduction

### Benefits:
