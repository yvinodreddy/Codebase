## MEETING PROCESS

### 30-Minute Intro Call Agenda:

**0-5 mins:** Introduction and rapport building
- Thank them for their time
- Acknowledge their specific achievement
- Share brief personal background

**5-15 mins:** Project overview
- RAG Heat and SWARMCARE vision
- Target impact and market
- Current progress and team
- United Health Group opportunity

**15-22 mins:** Specific questions for them
- [Prepare 3-5 specific questions based on their expertise]
- Take detailed notes

**22-28 mins:** Advisory board opportunity
- Explain board structure and expectations
- Time commitment and compensation
- Benefits for them

**28-30 mins:** Next steps
- Gauge interest level
- Schedule follow-up if interested
- Share additional materials
- Thank you

---

### 60-Minute Deep-Dive Meeting Agenda:

**0-5 mins:** Recap and agenda

**5-30 mins:** Product demo
- Live demonstration of working features
- Highlight areas relevant to their expertise
- Technical architecture overview (if technical advisor)

**30-45 mins:** Strategic discussion
- Their thoughts on approach
- Suggestions and feedback
- Potential challenges and solutions

**45-55 mins:** Advisory board details
- Formal proposal (equity or cash compensation)
- Time commitment and responsibilities
- Meeting schedule and format
- Legal agreements (NDA, advisory agreement)

**55-60 mins:** Decision timeline and next steps
