  How to Use for Next Campaign

  Simple 6-Step Process:

  1. Navigate to folder:
  cd /home/user01/claude-test/Exam/production/campaign
  2. Clear previous tracking:
  > data/sent_emails.txt
  3. Update config.json with new campaign details:
    - Email credentials
    - Template variables (org name, URL, deadline, etc.)
  4. Update data/candidates.csv with new recipients:
  Name,Email
  John Doe,john@example.com
  Jane Smith,jane@example.com
  5. Test first:
  python3 send_campaign.py --test-email your@email.com
  6. Run campaign:
  python3 send_campaign.py --send-all
